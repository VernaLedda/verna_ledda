> Crazy Crates


Image on the left is the developer section, right is at the start of the game

This is a simple game developed using Unity 3D. The goal of the game is to collect crates spread accross the game area without getting hit by the moving plates with sharp edges. Players shoul also keep themselves from hitting the walls or else the game over. I did the programming and 3D modelling and groupmates helped me with the design and the concept. This was our final project in our elective course in Game Development and was inspired by the popular games before Flappy bird and Temple Run.

> Web Portal of the Municipality of Santol, La Union


Image on the left is the home page, right is the Interactive map
(you can click the images to open the documentation file)

The Web Portal of the Municipality of Santol, La Union is a web-based system designed to help in terms of promotion of tourism in the town, connection between the officials and the residents, and distribution of information about the municipality.

The developed system provides the following functionalities: (a) manage records, (b) tourist spots, (c) gallery, (d) products, (e) awards and citation, (f) services offered, (g) schools, (h) establishments, (i) job opportunities, (j) hotlines, (k) contacts, (l) interactive map, (m) online forum and poll, (n) create accounts, (o) downloading and uploading files and forms, (p) latest news, (q) events, and (r) messaging.

The Web Portal of the Municipality of Santol, La Union has three types of users: administrator, registered users and visitors. The Oracle Portal Development Life Cycle method was utilized in the development of the Web Portal of the Municipality of Santol, La Union which has the following phases: architecture phase, development phase, staging phase and production phase.

This was our Capstone project, we took BS in Information Technology and were allowed to choose our groupmates (4 members). I worked with my closest friends and did majority of the coding while my groupmates helped me with the HTML parts and the graphics used. I remember putting all the efforts in this project because it is one of our major tickets to graduation, it wasn't an easy ride but I enjoyed it the trips to Santol to gather requirements, interviews and the overnights with my friends.
