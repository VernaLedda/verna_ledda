Home is where the heart is. It's the place where I get the best relaxation and peace of mind. I grew up with my family in La Union, a province located up north in Luzon island and I am thankful that its is not yet urbanized. We still still the fresh air and the laid-back life away from the busy hustles of the city.

When I decided to start my journey here in Canada I was sure I will miss home, and sure, yes, I miss it everyday. It serves as my motivation, my heart always years to the day I will return home to reunite with my family specially, with my parents and my siblings.
